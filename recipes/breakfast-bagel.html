Ingredients

  • Bacon 2 slices
  • Egg 1-2
  • Bagel
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Set a stove eye to medium heat and allow a few minutes for your pan to heat up
  2. Apply a thin coat of butter to the pan, then crack your egg(s) and monitor
  3. Prepare your bacon! I use a microwave safe plate, a few paper towels and 1-2 minuets checking between until crispy, but people with more time can use an oven at 350 for ~30 minutes
  4. Place your bagel into a toaster and use your preferred setting to toast. I prefer toasting at the 4th setting!
  5. Around the time your bagel is perfectly toasted it should be time to flip your egg!
  6. Cheese up the top half of your bagel, this will give it a few minutes to become melty!
  7. Your egg should be done, assuming its no-longer translucent! Apply salt & pepper to taste.
  8. Carefully I said carefully move your egg(s) to your beautifully toasted bagel
  9. Arrange your bacon, then... Crown.Your.King!
